How to Play

Roll the Dice
Tap the dice on your turn — or shake your phone! Move your token forward the number of squares shown.
Climb Ladders
Land on the bottom of a ladder and zoom up to a higher square. Lucky you!
Dodge Snakes
Land on a snake's head and slide all the way down to its tail. Oops!
Reach the Finish
First to land on the last square wins! You need to roll the exact number — too many and you bounce back.

Privacy Policy

My Snakes & Ladders

Last updated: March 2026

No data collected

This app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no internet connection required.

Local storage only

The app saves your game progress, custom boards, and high scores locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

No third parties

This app does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or social media plugins.

Children

This app is designed for all ages including children. Because no data is collected, there are no special privacy concerns for child users.
